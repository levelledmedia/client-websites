Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical driveway installation take?

Most driveway installations take between 3-7 days, depending on the size, design complexity, and weather conditions. We'll provide you with a detailed timeline during your free consultation.

Do you offer free quotes and site surveys?

Yes, we provide completely free, no-obligation quotes and comprehensive site surveys. Our experts will visit your property, discuss your requirements, and provide detailed recommendations with transparent pricing.

What areas do you cover?

We serve Luton and all surrounding areas in Bedfordshire. Contact us to discuss your project location and we'll be happy to help.

What types of paving materials do you offer?

We offer a wide range of materials including block paving, natural stone, porcelain, resin bound surfaces, tarmac, and pattern imprinted concrete. Each material has its own benefits, and we'll help you choose the best option for your project and budget.

Do I need planning permission for my driveway or patio?

In most cases, you won't need planning permission for driveways or patios. However, there are exceptions for certain properties or when using non-permeable materials. We'll advise you on any requirements during your consultation.

How do I maintain my new driveway or patio?

Maintenance requirements vary by material. Generally, regular sweeping, occasional washing, and weed prevention will keep your paving looking great. We provide detailed aftercare instructions and can offer professional cleaning and sealing services.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, bank transfer, and all major credit/debit cards. For larger projects, we offer flexible payment plans with staged payments aligned to project milestones.
